The 5 Core Features
1

Revenue-proportional contributions

You contribute 3% of monthly revenue. Lean months cost less. Growing months help the circle more. The pool breathes with your business, not against it.

2

On-time contributions build private trust

Your monthly payment history helps Pardna understand how consistently you participate in the circle. That record stays private to you and administrators inside the platform.

3

Industry-specific circles

You share a pool with people who understand your cash flow cycle. Shared industry context creates shared accountability — and shared patience when things get hard.

4

No-shame access, always

Applying for funds is not admitting failure. It is using what you built. The language, process, and culture of this app reflect the susu and pardna tradition: taking your turn is part of belonging.

5

Repayment is contribution — nothing more

When you apply for funds, you don’t receive a loan document or payment schedule. You continue contributing every month — and that is it. The same way these circles have worked for a thousand years.
